We also now offer small scale CNC!

Simply put, CNC stands for computer numerical control. What this means for you is our ability to do small parts machining using our desktop Nomad 883 CNC mill to create such things as circuit boards, keychains, dog tags and other small items. Even engraving can be done!

The materials we can cut include but are not limited to; plastics, soft and hard woods, aluminum, brass, copper, and in some cases even steel!

If you have some ideas on how to turn small pieces of wood, plastic or metal into something useful, please get in touch and we can help turn them into something special.
